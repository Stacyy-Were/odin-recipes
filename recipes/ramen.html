Tonkotsu Ramen

Ingredients

  • 3 Â½ cups Tonkotsu broth
  • 1 pack of ramen noodles
  • 2 tea spoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tea spoon of Habanero sauce
  • 1 tea spoon of Lulu sweet chilli
  • Desired amount of green onions
  • 3 boiled eggs

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, combine the Tonkotsu broth and the noodles on low heat

  2. In a bowl, mix the soy sauce and ramen seasoning until it lathers and becomes foamy

  3. Deep fry the green onions and add them to the bowl and mix
