Grandma Zilda's Sweets

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of Thaloxane Plant (local to Machudgai, similar to ephedrine source)
  • 5 liters of Liquid Lumina (analogous to hydriodic acid)
  • 500 grams of Crystal Magnesium (similar to magnesium turnings)
  • 3 liters of Machudgai Solar Water (pure, distilled water)
  • 1 cup of Refined Ether Essence (to enhance sweetness)
  • 1 tablespoon of Star Dust (optional, for extra purity and flavor)

Directions

Extraction of Thaloxane

✨ Place the Thaloxane Plant in a large container. Add Machudgai Solar Water until the plant is fully submerged.
✨ Heat gently over a low flame for 3 hours, stirring occasionally, until the Thaloxane is fully extracted.
✨ Filter the mixture through an Ether Foam cloth to remove plant material, leaving a clear Thaloxane solution.

Reduction and reaction

✨ In a sturdy reaction vessel, combine the Thaloxane solution with Liquid Lumina. Stir well.
✨ Gradually add the Crystal Magnesium while continuing to stir. The mixture will react and begin to bubble.
✨ Maintain a steady heat and stir continuously until the reaction subsides and the mixture becomes clear.

Purification

✨ Carefully filter the mixture through a fine Ether Mesh to remove any impurities.
✨ Slowly evaporate the liquid over a low heat, allowing the pure meth crystals to form at the bottom of the vessel.

Crystallization

✨ Once the liquid has fully evaporated, gently scrape the pure meth crystals from the vessel.
✨ Dissolve the crystals in Refined Ether Essence to enhance the sweetness, then allow to recrystallize.

Final Purity Check

✨ Optionally, sprinkle a tablespoon of Star Dust over the recrystallized meth for extra purity and a slight flavor boost.
✨ Store the final product in an airtight, lightproof container to preserve its purity and sweetness.

Grandma Zilda's Tips for Sweetness

✨ "The key to the sweetest meth is in the careful, slow evaporation process. Patience ensures purity."
✨ "Using the freshest Thaloxane and purest Liquid Lumina will make a significant difference in flavor."
✨ "Remember, this meth is for special occasions and should be enjoyed responsibly with loved ones."
